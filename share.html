Community Guidelines

To ensure a positive experience for all users, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Be respectful to other authors and readers
  • Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
  • Properly tag mature content
  • Give credit for any borrowed ideas or content
  • Engage constructively with feedback and comments

Tips for Sharing Your Story

  1. Polish your work before sharing - proofread and edit thoroughly
  2. Write an engaging synopsis to hook potential readers
  3. Choose the right genre and tags to help readers find your story
  4. Engage with your readers through comments and discussions
  5. Share your story on social media to attract more readers
  6. Consider joining writing groups for support and feedback
  7. Be open to constructive criticism and use it to improve your writing
